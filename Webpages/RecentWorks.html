Guttergold: Something from Nothing

"Between the road and the footpath there exists a hidden universe: a concrete shoreline collecting the debris of the ever-expanding ocean of urban life. Like a coast on every roadway it is here that the fragments of the surrounding world pool and gather. On this kerbside stage daily interactions between humans, animals, and the environment are played out in bitumen-coated melodrama of suburban existence."

Adrienne Kenafake, 2014
